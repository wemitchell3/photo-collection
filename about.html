Billy Mitchell Bio

I was born in Atlanta, GA on Oct. 18th, 1979, which makes me a Libra. My family moved back to the Nashville, TN area, Nolensville to be exact, when I was only 2 years old. I went to Nolensville elementary school through 5th grade, Ezell Harding for 6th grade, Battle Ground Academy for 7th – 10th, and Centennial High School for 11th and 12th. I graduated high school in 1998 and went to UT Knoxville for a year and then took a couple of years off school to work. I moved back to the greater Nashville area in 2002 and finished my college degree in Business Administration from MTSU in fall of 2005. I immediately began work with Dell Inc. where I worked for about 6.5 years initial selling basic laptops and desktops. I soon began selling servers, storage, and network equipment to large data centers. I left Dell in Nov. of 2012 for the mortgage business as a career change. I found within 3 years the mortgage business just wasn’t really for me. After leaving the mortgage business, I began looking for careers in technology and ended up starting an online retail business selling business and consumer electronics and technology. After three years I decided I wanted to learn computer programing, so I am not attending NSS.
